**Scenario Title**

Researching Refund Options After a Cancelled Vocational Course

**Industry / Practice Area**

Education / Consumer Training

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Legal Research Note

# Scenario Background

Maya paid for a part-time vocational course advertised as including a final practical workshop. The provider cancelled the workshop after losing access to its venue and offered only online recorded materials. Maya wants to know whether she can seek a partial refund or a replacement practical session.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal researcher. You have been asked to prepare a practical research note for a supervising solicitor. Your role is to identify the research question, use the provided source extracts carefully, avoid invented authorities, and present a cautious practical conclusion.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Supervisor Research Request
1. Exhibit 2 - Factual Background Note
1. Exhibit 3 - Neutral Legal Principles Extract
1. Exhibit 4 - Example Decision / Dispute Summary
1. Exhibit 5 - Practical Guidance Extract
1. Exhibit 6 - Conflicting Commentary or Policy Extract

# Core Skill Task

Carry out focused, source-led legal research and produce a practical research note for a supervisor.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare a legal research note answering: What practical refund or replacement-course position can Maya put forward when the provider cancelled the final practical module? The note must be practical, jurisdiction-neutral, and based on the provided source extracts.
