# Detailed Practice Tasks

Task 1 - Identify the parties, the negotiation context, and Orchard's immediate objective.

Task 2 - Extract the key facts and documents that affect negotiation leverage.

Task 3 - Prepare a short chronology of the events leading to the current dispute.

Task 4 - Separate confirmed facts from allegations, assumptions, disputed points, and missing evidence.

Task 5 - Identify Orchard's commercial priorities, red lines, and areas where flexibility may be sensible.

Task 6 - Analyse the main legal, commercial, practical, and relationship risks affecting the negotiation with RapidRoute.

Task 7 - Identify further information, evidence, and supervisor instructions needed before any offer is made.

Task 8 - Plan a negotiation strategy, including opening position, fallback position, possible concessions, and non-financial terms.

Task 9 - Identify communication risks, tone issues, and wording that should be avoided during the negotiation.

Task 10 - Prepare the final negotiation plan that will support the live negotiation and the post-negotiation record.
