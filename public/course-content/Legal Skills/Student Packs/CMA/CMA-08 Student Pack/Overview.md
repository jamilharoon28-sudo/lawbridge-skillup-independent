*Analysing a Clinic Equipment Lease and Service Failure*

**Scenario Title** Analysing a Clinic Equipment Lease and Service Failure

**Industry / Practice Area** Healthcare / Equipment Leasing / Service Contracts

**Difficulty** Intermediate

**Scenario Type** Case and matter analysis simulation

# Scenario Background

Maple Grove Diagnostics Ltd has asked for help with faulty diagnostic scanner, lease payments and service response delays. Dr Aisha Bernard, Clinical Director, wants the legal team to help prepare a matter analysis note identifying practical options and evidence gaps. The clinic is losing appointments and wants to stop lease payments while publicly warning other clinics. The available file is incomplete and contains conflicting accounts, so the student must analyse the matter carefully before any external communication or recommendation is made.

Central tension: The clinic says the scanner is unusable; the supplier says errors are caused by staff calibration and room temperature issues. The matter cannot be resolved by accepting either side's account without further evidence.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal professional in the team. You report to Ravi Patel. You are not expected to make final strategic decisions or send external correspondence. Your task is to analyse the matter file, organise the evidence, identify strengths and weaknesses, and prepare a clear note for supervisor review.

Analyse a realistic matter file, identify the key issues and evidence gaps, and prepare a practical case/matter analysis note for a supervising solicitor or matter lead.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare a concise case/matter analysis note for Ravi Patel. Your note should summarise the client objective, key facts, issue list, strengths, weaknesses, evidence gaps, communication risks, recommended next steps, and decisions requiring supervisor input.
