*Jurisdiction-neutral practical drafting simulation*

**Scenario Title** Drafting a Data Handling Addendum for a Marketing Platform

**Industry / Practice Area** E-commerce / Marketing Technology / Data Handling

**Client** Orchid Lane Homeware

**Counterparty / Other Party** PulseSend Marketing Ltd

**Core Drafting Output** data handling addendum and approval paragraph for a marketing platform renewal

# Scenario Background:

Orchid Lane Homeware uses PulseSend Marketing Ltd for customer email campaigns. A renewal is due, and the ecommerce team wants a short addendum before uploading a new customer segment. PulseSend’s standard terms are brief and do not set out incident notification or deletion details. The client wants strong wording, but the commercial team needs the renewal signed quickly before a promotional campaign. The drafting task is to produce practical, jurisdiction-neutral data handling wording for supervisor review.

# Student Role:

You are a junior legal professional assisting Sofia Marin, supervising solicitor. You have been asked to prepare the first working draft of the data handling addendum and approval paragraph for a marketing platform renewal. You are not expected to give final legal advice or send the document. Your role is to organise the facts, identify drafting risks, prepare clear wording, and highlight points requiring supervisor approval.

# Core Skill Task:

Use the documents to prepare a clear, evidence-based, proportionate legal draft that reflects the client’s instructions while avoiding unsupported admissions, overstatement, ambiguity, and jurisdiction-specific assumptions.

# Main Practical Task:

Draft a data handling addendum that sets clear processing, security, incident notice, and deletion obligations without citing jurisdiction-specific data protection law.
