**Scenario Title**

Writing a Client Advice Email About a Cancelled Online Furniture Order

**Industry / Practice Area**

Consumer / E-commerce

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Client Advice Email

# Scenario Background

Amira ordered a dining table from HomeNest Online for a new flat. The delivery was rearranged three times, a damaged table was offered as a substitute, and Amira now wants a clear written explanation of her options before she replies to the retailer. She has drafted an angry email but the evidence does not yet support every allegation she wants to make.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal team member. You have been asked to prepare a professional written communication using the six exhibits. Your role is to organise the information, write clearly for the intended audience, and avoid unsupported conclusions.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Client Request Email
1. Exhibit 2 - Order and Payment Summary
1. Exhibit 3 - Delivery Timeline
1. Exhibit 4 - Retailer Response
1. Exhibit 5 - Supervisor Instruction
1. Exhibit 6 - Amira Draft Email

# Core Skill Task

Write a clear, accurate, audience-appropriate client advice email that uses the documents carefully and reflects uncertainty where needed.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare the final client advice email for client. The communication should be clear, calm, practical, client-friendly, and measured and should help the reader understand the practical next step.
