# Scenario Title:

Analysing an EdTech Pilot Conversion With School Districts

# Industry / Practice Area:

Education technology / public-sector procurement / subscriptions

# Scenario Background:

LearnLoop Analytics is considering whether to offer discounted multi-year terms with expanded reporting features. The potential value or exposure is approximately USD 780,000 three-year subscription value. The commercial team wants a practical answer by the district procurement call on Thursday. The business sees opportunity, but the materials show legal, operational, financial, and communication risks. The key tension is that the pilot success metrics are disputed and the requested reporting features are not fully built The learner must prepare commercial legal analysis that helps the business decide what to do next without overstating the evidence or ignoring commercial priorities.

# Student Role:

You are a junior legal team member assisting the supervising solicitor. You have been asked to review the documents, identify the legal and commercial risks, prepare a business-facing analysis, and support a discussion with the business stakeholder. You are not expected to make the final business decision.

# Core Skill Task:

Analyse a commercial problem through a legal lens and translate the risks into practical business options, safeguards, and next steps.

# Main Practical Task:

Prepare a commercial legal risk note for Sarah Kim, explaining the key risks, options, safeguards, missing evidence, and recommended next steps on conversion of school analytics pilot into paid multi-year subscription. Use the note to support a business briefing conversation.
