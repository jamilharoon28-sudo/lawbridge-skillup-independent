**Scenario Title**

Researching Safeguarding Disclosure in a Volunteer Charity Setting

**Industry / Practice Area**

Charities / Safeguarding

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Legal Research Note

# Scenario Background

A youth charity received an internal concern about a volunteer who also helps at another organisation. The concern is not fully investigated. The operations lead wants to warn the other organisation immediately.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal researcher. You have been asked to prepare a practical research note for a supervising solicitor. Your role is to identify the research question, use the provided source extracts carefully, avoid invented authorities, and present a cautious practical conclusion.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Supervisor Research Request
1. Exhibit 2 - Factual Background Note
1. Exhibit 3 - Neutral Legal Principles Extract
1. Exhibit 4 - Example Decision / Dispute Summary
1. Exhibit 5 - Practical Guidance Extract
1. Exhibit 6 - Conflicting Commentary or Policy Extract

# Core Skill Task

Carry out focused, source-led legal research and produce a practical research note for a supervisor.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare a legal research note answering: What practical research points should be considered before advising on disclosure of a safeguarding concern to another organisation? The note must be practical, jurisdiction-neutral, and based on the provided source extracts.
