# AI Skills

## AI Skill 1

Use AI to generate a first-pass chronology for a dispute with a departing co-founder over code access and customer contacts, then check it against the exhibits and correct any assumed or unsupported details.

## AI Skill 2

Ask AI to separate confirmed facts from allegations or assumptions. Review the output and identify at least three points where human judgment is needed.

## AI Skill 3

Give AI the client's draft communication and ask it to identify overstatement, tone risk, and missing caveats. Revise the output so it remains evidence-led and supervisor-safe.

## AI Section Completion Standard:

A good response uses AI to organise and test the materials, but does not let AI invent facts, decide strategy, contact the client, or replace professional judgment. The student should check the AI output against the exhibits and explain where supervisor review is needed.
