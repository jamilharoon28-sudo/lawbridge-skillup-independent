# AI Skills

### AI Skill 1 — AI issue map

Use AI to organise the facts into ownership, payment, scope, investor-risk, and evidence-gap categories, then verify every entry against an exhibit.

### AI Skill 2 — Missing evidence list

Ask AI to create a missing document checklist for the client interview, then refine it so it asks for precise documents rather than broad categories.

### AI Skill 3 — Overconfident AI critique

Critique an AI-generated statement that says “FitLoop clearly owns all code because Nina was a freelancer” and replace it with safer wording.

### AI Section Completion Standard

A good response uses AI to organise, test and improve preparation, but checks every AI output against the exhibits, preserves uncertainty, and leaves legal judgment and external communication decisions for supervised human review.
