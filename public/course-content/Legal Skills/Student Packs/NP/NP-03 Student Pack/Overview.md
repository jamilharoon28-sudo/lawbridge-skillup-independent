# Scenario Title:

Negotiating Renewal Terms After a SaaS Service Failure

# Industry / Practice Area:

Technology / SaaS / data access

# Scenario Background:

HarbourGate Clinics Group is preparing for a negotiation with CloudDesk Software Inc. about a SaaS renewal, service credits, and data export dispute. The client wants to avoid automatic renewal on unfavourable terms while securing continuity, data access, and a fair credit for service failures. The business is under time pressure because the issue needs movement by before the current licence renews on 30 April. The documents show useful leverage, but they also reveal uncertainty, evidence gaps, and facts that may weaken the client's position. The task is to prepare a balanced negotiation plan that protects the client's position without overstating liability or creating unnecessary relationship damage.

# Student Role:

You are a junior legal team member assisting the supervising solicitor. You have been asked to review the documents, prepare the negotiation strategy, identify missing evidence, and support a live negotiation. You are not expected to make final settlement decisions without supervisor approval.

# Documents / Information Available:

1. Exhibit 1 - Client Instruction Email
1. Exhibit 2 - Contract or Terms Extract
1. Exhibit 3 - Chronology and Evidence Summary
1. Exhibit 4 - Counterparty Position Note
1. Exhibit 5 - Commercial Risk and Cost Note
1. Exhibit 6 - Draft Negotiation Email Prepared by Client

# Core Skill Task:

Prepare for a negotiation by identifying objectives, leverage, risks, fallback positions, concessions, and communication boundaries.

# Main Practical Task:

Prepare a negotiation strategy note for the supervising solicitor and use it to conduct a focused negotiation with CloudDesk. After the negotiation, record the agreed position, unresolved points, and recommended next steps.
