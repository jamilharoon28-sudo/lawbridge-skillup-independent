**Scenario Title**

Writing a Settlement Email About a Supplier Shortfall

**Industry / Practice Area**

Food Manufacturing / Supply Chain

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Settlement Proposal Email

# Scenario Background

A food manufacturer wants to preserve a supplier relationship after short deliveries affected production. The documents show supply issues, but also forecast changes and unresolved loss calculations.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal team member. You have been asked to prepare a professional written communication using the six exhibits. Your role is to organise the information, write clearly for the intended audience, and avoid unsupported conclusions.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Client Request
1. Exhibit 2 - Delivery and Order Summary
1. Exhibit 3 - Supply Terms Extract
1. Exhibit 4 - GlassPro Email
1. Exhibit 5 - Finance Note
1. Exhibit 6 - Draft Settlement Email

# Core Skill Task

Write a clear, accurate, audience-appropriate settlement proposal email that uses the documents carefully and reflects uncertainty where needed.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare the final settlement proposal email for supplier counterparty. The communication should be commercial, constructive, accurate, and solution-focused and should help the reader understand the practical next step.
