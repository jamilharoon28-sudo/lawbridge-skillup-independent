# AI Skills

### AI Skill 1 — AI chronology check

Use AI to convert the six exhibits into a draft chronology, then check every entry against the source document and mark uncertain dates.

### AI Skill 2 — Interview question categories

Ask AI to suggest interview question categories for Maya, then remove any questions that sound leading, insensitive, or like legal advice.

### AI Skill 3 — Tone risk review

Give AI Maya’s draft message in summary form and ask it to identify overstatement or risky wording, then write safer neutral wording for supervisor review.

### AI Section Completion Standard

A good response uses AI to organise, test and improve preparation, but checks every AI output against the exhibits, preserves uncertainty, and leaves legal judgment and external communication decisions for supervised human review.
