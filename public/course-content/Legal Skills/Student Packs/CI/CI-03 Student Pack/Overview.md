**Scenario Title**

Interviewing a Startup Founder About a Contractor Claiming Ownership of App Code

**Industry / Practice Area**

Technology startup / software development / intellectual property and commercial risk

## Scenario Background

Leo Patel founded a fitness app startup. A former developer, Nina Brooks, has demanded that the startup stop using parts of the app code unless she is paid more and credited as co-founder. Leo says she was “just a freelancer”, but the documents show informal messages, no fully signed services agreement, and possible scope changes. Leo wants the firm to tell Nina she has no rights before an investor demo. The student must prepare to interview Leo, clarify the factual and commercial position, and avoid overstating ownership or advising on rights before key documents are reviewed.

## Student Role

You are acting as a trainee solicitor or junior legal professional preparing for an initial client interview. Your task is to organise the information, prepare a structured interview, avoid premature advice, and support the supervising solicitor after the interview.
