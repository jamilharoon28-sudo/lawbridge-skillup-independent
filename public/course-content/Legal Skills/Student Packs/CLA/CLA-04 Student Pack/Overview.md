# Scenario Title:

Assessing a Private Clinic Device Partnership Under Revenue Pressure

# Industry / Practice Area:

Healthcare services / medical devices / commercial partnership

# Scenario Background:

Asterwell Clinics is considering whether to enter an exclusive device rollout partnership. The potential value or exposure is approximately USD 950,000 first-year rollout cost. The commercial team wants a practical answer by the board investment session next Monday. The business sees opportunity, but the materials show legal, operational, financial, and communication risks. The key tension is that the clinical pilot feedback is positive but patient consent process, support cover, and data reporting remain unresolved The learner must prepare commercial legal analysis that helps the business decide what to do next without overstating the evidence or ignoring commercial priorities.

# Student Role:

You are a junior legal team member assisting the supervising solicitor. You have been asked to review the documents, identify the legal and commercial risks, prepare a business-facing analysis, and support a discussion with the business stakeholder. You are not expected to make the final business decision.

# Documents / Information Available:

1. Exhibit 1 - Business Instruction Email
1. Exhibit 2 - Proposed Commercial Terms Extract
1. Exhibit 3 - Operational Data and Timeline Note
1. Exhibit 4 - Internal Stakeholder Message Thread
1. Exhibit 5 - Risk, Finance, and Options Note
1. Exhibit 6 - Draft Board or Customer Communication

# Core Skill Task:

Analyse a commercial problem through a legal lens and translate the risks into practical business options, safeguards, and next steps.

# Main Practical Task:

Prepare a commercial legal risk note for Dr Lena Ortiz, explaining the key risks, options, safeguards, missing evidence, and recommended next steps on device partnership for patient monitoring. Use the note to support a business briefing conversation.
