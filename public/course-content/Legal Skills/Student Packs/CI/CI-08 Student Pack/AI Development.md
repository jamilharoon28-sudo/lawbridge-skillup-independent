# AI Skills

### AI Skill 1 — Allegation/evidence table

Use AI to turn the facts into allegation, supporting evidence, missing evidence, and immediate action categories.

### AI Skill 2 — Sensitive question wording

Ask AI to draft questions about Oliver’s mother’s condition and capacity, then edit for sensitivity and clarity.

### AI Skill 3 — Unsafe public post critique

Critique an AI-drafted social media complaint for unsupported accusations and rewrite as a neutral evidence-preservation note.

### AI Section Completion Standard

A good response uses AI to organise, test and improve preparation, but checks every AI output against the exhibits, preserves uncertainty, and leaves legal judgment and external communication decisions for supervised human review.
