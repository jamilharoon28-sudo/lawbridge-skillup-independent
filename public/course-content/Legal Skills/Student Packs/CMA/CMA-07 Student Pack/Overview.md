*Analysing an Influencer Campaign Breakdown*

**Scenario Title** Analysing an Influencer Campaign Breakdown

**Industry / Practice Area** Media / Influencer Marketing / Brand Partnerships

**Difficulty** Intermediate

**Scenario Type** Case and matter analysis simulation

# Scenario Background

LumaSkin Cosmetics Ltd has asked for help with missed social media campaign deliverables and disputed approval obligations. Harriet Zhou, Marketing Director, wants the legal team to help identify strengths, weaknesses, evidence gaps and communication risks before a response is drafted. The campaign tied to a product launch has underperformed and the client wants a refund and public correction. The available file is incomplete and contains conflicting accounts, so the student must analyse the matter carefully before any external communication or recommendation is made.

Central tension: The brand says posts were late and off-brief, while the agency says the brand delayed approvals and changed messaging. The matter cannot be resolved by accepting either side's account without further evidence.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal professional in the team. You report to Naomi Brooks. You are not expected to make final strategic decisions or send external correspondence. Your task is to analyse the matter file, organise the evidence, identify strengths and weaknesses, and prepare a clear note for supervisor review.

Analyse a realistic matter file, identify the key issues and evidence gaps, and prepare a practical case/matter analysis note for a supervising solicitor or matter lead.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare a concise case/matter analysis note for Naomi Brooks. Your note should summarise the client objective, key facts, issue list, strengths, weaknesses, evidence gaps, communication risks, recommended next steps, and decisions requiring supervisor input.
