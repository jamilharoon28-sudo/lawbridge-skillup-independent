# AI Skills

### AI Skill 1 — AI chronology with evidence tags

Use AI to build a project timeline, but add exhibit references and mark unsupported notebook-only entries.

### AI Skill 2 — Commercial objective questions

Ask AI to draft interview questions about payment, cash flow and relationship risk, then refine them so they remain neutral.

### AI Skill 3 — Draft threat review

Use AI to identify risky wording in a proposed “we will stop work” message and produce safer issues-for-supervisor wording.

### AI Section Completion Standard

A good response uses AI to organise, test and improve preparation, but checks every AI output against the exhibits, preserves uncertainty, and leaves legal judgment and external communication decisions for supervised human review.
