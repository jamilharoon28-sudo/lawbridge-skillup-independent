# Exhibit 1 — Supervisor Research Request

**Document type** Supervisor instruction

**Status** Training exhibit / fictional workplace document

The supervising solicitor asks for a concise research note answering: What practical research points should be considered before advising on disclosure of a safeguarding concern to another organisation?

The note must be jurisdiction-neutral, practical, and based only on the provided source extracts unless further research is authorised. It should identify any further documents needed before advice is finalised.

### Exhibit Questions

1. Identify the key facts, dates, people, and documents in this exhibit.
1. Explain how this exhibit affects the main practical task, including what it proves, what remains uncertain, and what should be followed up.
