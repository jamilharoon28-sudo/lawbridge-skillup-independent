**Scenario Title**

Writing a Client Update About a Landlord Repair Complaint

**Industry / Practice Area**

Housing / Property Management

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Client Update Email

# Scenario Background

A tenant wants an update after sending photographs and message records about damp. The client is anxious and wants to stop paying rent, but the documents show uncertainty about cause, access, and medical evidence.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal team member. You have been asked to prepare a professional written communication using the six exhibits. Your role is to organise the information, write clearly for the intended audience, and avoid unsupported conclusions.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Client Update Request
1. Exhibit 2 - Photo and Message Timeline
1. Exhibit 3 - Tenancy Repair Extract
1. Exhibit 4 - Managing Agent Email
1. Exhibit 5 - Supervisor Instruction
1. Exhibit 6 - Rosa Draft Message

# Core Skill Task

Write a clear, accurate, audience-appropriate client update email that uses the documents carefully and reflects uncertainty where needed.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare the final client update email for tenant client. The communication should be supportive, practical, clear, and careful and should help the reader understand the practical next step.
