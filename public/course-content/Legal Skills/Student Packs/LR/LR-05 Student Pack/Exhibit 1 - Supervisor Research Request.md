# Exhibit 1 — Supervisor Research Request

**Document type** Supervisor instruction

**Status** Training exhibit / fictional workplace document

The note must be jurisdiction-neutral, practical, and based only on the provided source extracts unless further research is authorised. It should identify any further documents needed before advice is finalised.
