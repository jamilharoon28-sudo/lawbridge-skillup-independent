**Scenario Title**

Writing a Clinic Complaint Response About a Missed Appointment and Data Error

**Industry / Practice Area**

Healthcare Administration

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Complaint Response Letter

# Scenario Background

A private clinic received a complaint from a patient after a cancelled consultation and an email sent to the wrong address. The clinic wants a response that is empathetic, accurate, and careful about what is confirmed.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal team member. You have been asked to prepare a professional written communication using the six exhibits. Your role is to organise the information, write clearly for the intended audience, and avoid unsupported conclusions.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Patient Complaint
1. Exhibit 2 - Appointment Log
1. Exhibit 3 - Email Audit Extract
1. Exhibit 4 - Reception Note
1. Exhibit 5 - Clinic Complaints Procedure
1. Exhibit 6 - Draft Response

# Core Skill Task

Write a clear, accurate, audience-appropriate complaint response letter that uses the documents carefully and reflects uncertainty where needed.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare the final complaint response letter for patient complainant. The communication should be empathetic, accountable where facts are confirmed, clear, and careful and should help the reader understand the practical next step.
