# Detailed Practice Tasks

Task 1 - Identify the business context, the parties, and the decision Oakridge must make.

Task 2 - Extract the key commercial facts, figures, deadlines, and documents from the exhibits.

Task 3 - Map the main legal, commercial, operational, reputational, and stakeholder risks.

Task 4 - Separate confirmed facts from assumptions, disputed points, forecasts, and missing evidence.

Task 5 - Identify Oakridge's commercial objectives, constraints, and non-legal priorities.

Task 6 - Assess the practical options available to the business, including proceeding, pausing, renegotiating, or approving with conditions.

Task 7 - Identify the further information, evidence, and internal approvals needed before a final decision is made.

Task 8 - Plan how to explain the legal risk in business-friendly language without overstating certainty.

Task 9 - Identify communication risks, including statements, commitments, or commercial claims that should not be made yet.

Task 10 - Prepare the structure and key points for a commercial legal risk note to the business decision-maker.
