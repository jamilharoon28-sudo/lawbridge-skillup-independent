# Scenario Title:

Negotiating Rent Arrears and a Revised Exit Plan for a Boutique Tenant

# Industry / Practice Area:

Property / retail lease management

# Scenario Background:

Luma Lane Boutique is preparing for a negotiation with Cedar Yard Estates about a rent arrears and lease exit negotiation. The client wants to agree a practical repayment or exit arrangement that avoids immediate enforcement and protects stock, reputation, and trading continuity. The business is under time pressure because the issue needs movement by before the landlord issues a formal demand next week. The documents show useful leverage, but they also reveal uncertainty, evidence gaps, and facts that may weaken the client's position. The task is to prepare a balanced negotiation plan that protects the client's position without overstating liability or creating unnecessary relationship damage.

# Student Role:

You are a junior legal team member assisting the supervising solicitor. You have been asked to review the documents, prepare the negotiation strategy, identify missing evidence, and support a live negotiation. You are not expected to make final settlement decisions without supervisor approval.

# Documents / Information Available:

1. Exhibit 1 - Client Instruction Email
1. Exhibit 2 - Contract or Terms Extract
1. Exhibit 3 - Chronology and Evidence Summary
1. Exhibit 4 - Counterparty Position Note
1. Exhibit 5 - Commercial Risk and Cost Note
1. Exhibit 6 - Draft Negotiation Email Prepared by Client

# Core Skill Task:

Prepare for a negotiation by identifying objectives, leverage, risks, fallback positions, concessions, and communication boundaries.

# Main Practical Task:

Prepare a negotiation strategy note for the supervising solicitor and use it to conduct a focused negotiation with Cedar Yard. After the negotiation, record the agreed position, unresolved points, and recommended next steps.
