**Scenario Title**

Writing a Red Flag Summary About an Influencer Sponsorship Dispute

**Industry / Practice Area**

Media / Influencer Marketing

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Red Flag Summary

# Scenario Background

An influencer agency needs a concise red flag summary for a brand client after a campaign underperformed. The brand wants a hard line, but the contract and messages show possible approval delays and unclear deliverables.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal team member. You have been asked to prepare a professional written communication using the six exhibits. Your role is to organise the information, write clearly for the intended audience, and avoid unsupported conclusions.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Client Request
1. Exhibit 2 - Campaign Brief
1. Exhibit 3 - Posting Timeline
1. Exhibit 4 - Message Thread
1. Exhibit 5 - Supervisor Instruction
1. Exhibit 6 - Brand Draft Complaint

# Core Skill Task

Write a clear, accurate, audience-appropriate red flag summary that uses the documents carefully and reflects uncertainty where needed.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare the final red flag summary for commercial client. The communication should be short, commercial, clear, and risk-focused and should help the reader understand the practical next step.
