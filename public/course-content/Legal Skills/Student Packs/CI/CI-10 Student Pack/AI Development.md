# AI Skills

### AI Skill 1 — Restriction issue map

Use AI to map funds, donor wording, proposed use, missing documents, and escalation points, then verify against the exhibits.

### AI Skill 2 — Board wording critique

Ask AI to identify risks in wording such as “internal bridging” and produce neutral questions for Adam to ask the board.

### AI Skill 3 — Confidential prompt design

Draft an AI prompt that redacts names and sensitive finances while asking for a structure for interview questions.

### AI Section Completion Standard

A good response uses AI to organise, test and improve preparation, but checks every AI output against the exhibits, preserves uncertainty, and leaves legal judgment and external communication decisions for supervised human review.
