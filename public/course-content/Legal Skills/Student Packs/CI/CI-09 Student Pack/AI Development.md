# AI Skills

### AI Skill 1 — Complaint classifier

Use AI to classify complaints by severity, batch, date, and evidence available, then check for missing data.

### AI Skill 2 — Customer email risk check

Ask AI to review the proposed reassurance email and identify unsupported safety claims.

### AI Skill 3 — Interview prompt refinement

Use AI to draft payment processor and product-safety interview questions, then make them neutral and evidence-led.

### AI Section Completion Standard

A good response uses AI to organise, test and improve preparation, but checks every AI output against the exhibits, preserves uncertainty, and leaves legal judgment and external communication decisions for supervised human review.
