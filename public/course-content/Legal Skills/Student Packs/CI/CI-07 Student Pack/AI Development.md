# AI Skills

### AI Skill 1 — Approval evidence map

Use AI to map which posts were approved, delayed, or changed, then check each entry against exhibits.

### AI Skill 2 — Public response risk review

Ask AI to identify reputational and overstatement risks in a draft public response, then produce safer holding wording.

### AI Skill 3 — Interview question generator

Use AI to generate categories for questions about campaign scope, deliverables, analytics, and payment, then remove repetitive or adversarial questions.

### AI Section Completion Standard

A good response uses AI to organise, test and improve preparation, but checks every AI output against the exhibits, preserves uncertainty, and leaves legal judgment and external communication decisions for supervised human review.
