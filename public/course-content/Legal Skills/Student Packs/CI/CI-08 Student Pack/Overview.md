**Interviewing a Family Member About a Care Home Complaint and Missing Property**

**Scenario Title**

Interviewing a Family Member About a Care Home Complaint and Missing Property

**Industry / Practice Area**

Health and social care / care home complaint / evidence and communication risk

## Scenario Background

Oliver Grant’s mother lives in Rosemere Care Home. Oliver says she has been left in soiled clothing and that jewellery has gone missing. The care home says staff responded appropriately and that some property was not listed on admission. Oliver wants to accuse the home of neglect and theft in writing. The materials show serious concerns but incomplete records, possible staff shortages, and uncertainty about property inventory. The student must prepare for a sensitive interview that separates allegations from evidence and identifies immediate safeguarding and evidence steps.

## Student Role

You are acting as a trainee solicitor or junior legal professional preparing for an initial client interview. Your task is to organise the information, prepare a structured interview, avoid premature advice, and support the supervising solicitor after the interview.

Conduct a structured client interview and produce a short post-interview summary for the supervising solicitor.
