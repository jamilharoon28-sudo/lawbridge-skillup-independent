# Exhibit 4 — Email chain about guest number changes

Email chain between Harbour House events team and Mason & Reed account manager.

On 20 February, Harbour House increased one event from 110 guests to 148 guests.

Mason & Reed responded that staffing would need to be rechecked.

On 23 February, Harbour House asked whether the extra staff were confirmed.

Mason & Reed replied that confirmation was expected the next morning.
