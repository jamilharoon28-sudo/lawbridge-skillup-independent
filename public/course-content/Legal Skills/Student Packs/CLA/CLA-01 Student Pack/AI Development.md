# AI Skills

AI Skill 1 - Use AI to create a first-pass risk matrix from the exhibits, then correct unsupported assumptions and add missing commercial context.

AI Skill 2 - Ask AI to translate legal and operational risks into business impact language for a non-lawyer stakeholder, then check that the wording remains accurate.

AI Skill 3 - Critique an AI-generated executive summary for overconfidence, invented facts, weak caveats, or failure to address Oakridge's commercial objective.

AI Section Completion Standard:

A good response uses AI to organise and test analysis, but verifies every point against the exhibits. It keeps legal and commercial judgment with the student, avoids invented facts, and translates risk into clear business options without giving false certainty.
