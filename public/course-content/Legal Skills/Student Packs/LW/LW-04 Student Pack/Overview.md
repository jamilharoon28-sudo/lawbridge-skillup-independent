**Scenario Title**

Writing an Opponent Letter About Delayed Cafe Fit-Out Works

**Industry / Practice Area**

Construction / Small Business

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Opponent Letter

# Scenario Background

A cafe owner wants a firm letter to the fit-out contractor after a delayed opening. The documents support delay and unresolved snagging, but also show changes to the design and one missed access date.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal team member. You have been asked to prepare a professional written communication using the six exhibits. Your role is to organise the information, write clearly for the intended audience, and avoid unsupported conclusions.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Client Instruction
1. Exhibit 2 - Works Timeline
1. Exhibit 3 - Contract Extract
1. Exhibit 4 - Snagging List
1. Exhibit 5 - Contractor Email
1. Exhibit 6 - Client Draft Letter

# Core Skill Task

Write a clear, accurate, audience-appropriate opponent letter that uses the documents carefully and reflects uncertainty where needed.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare the final opponent letter for contractor opponent. The communication should be firm, factual, proportionate, and non-inflammatory and should help the reader understand the practical next step.
