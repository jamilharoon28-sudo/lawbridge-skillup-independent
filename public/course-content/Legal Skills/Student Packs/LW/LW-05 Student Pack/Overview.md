**Scenario Title**

Writing a Board Briefing About a Software Rollout Failure

**Industry / Practice Area**

Technology / Retail Operations

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Board Briefing

# Scenario Background

A retail group board wants a clear written briefing before deciding whether to terminate a software rollout. Internal documents show vendor delay, but also client data issues and scope changes.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal team member. You have been asked to prepare a professional written communication using the six exhibits. Your role is to organise the information, write clearly for the intended audience, and avoid unsupported conclusions.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Board Request
1. Exhibit 2 - Rollout Chronology
1. Exhibit 3 - Implementation Agreement Extract
1. Exhibit 4 - Vendor Email
1. Exhibit 5 - Internal Operations Note
1. Exhibit 6 - Draft Board Slide

# Core Skill Task

Write a clear, accurate, audience-appropriate board briefing that uses the documents carefully and reflects uncertainty where needed.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare the final board briefing for client board. The communication should be business-facing, concise, neutral, and decision-focused and should help the reader understand the practical next step.
