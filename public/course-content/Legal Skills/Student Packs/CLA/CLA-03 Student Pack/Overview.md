# Scenario Title:

Analysing a Supplier Switch for Lower-Carbon Delivery Packaging

# Industry / Practice Area:

Logistics / sustainability / procurement

# Scenario Background:

FreshRoute Grocery Delivery is considering whether to replace the current insulated packaging supplier. The potential value or exposure is approximately USD 410,000 annual packaging commitment. The commercial team wants a practical answer by the quarterly sustainability committee. The business sees opportunity, but the materials show legal, operational, financial, and communication risks. The key tension is that ecocrate has strong sustainability claims but limited cold-chain performance evidence at freshroute volumes The learner must prepare commercial legal analysis that helps the business decide what to do next without overstating the evidence or ignoring commercial priorities.

# Student Role:

You are a junior legal team member assisting the supervising solicitor. You have been asked to review the documents, identify the legal and commercial risks, prepare a business-facing analysis, and support a discussion with the business stakeholder. You are not expected to make the final business decision.
