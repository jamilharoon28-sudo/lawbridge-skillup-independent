# AI Skills

## AI Skill 1 - Interview question planning

Use AI to generate an initial list of interview question categories for this matter. Review the output and identify which questions are useful, which are too leading, and which need to be rewritten in a more neutral client-friendly way.

## AI Skill 2 - Missing information check

Use AI to compare the six exhibits and suggest missing information or documents. Check the AI output against the exhibits and remove any points that are speculative or unsupported.

## AI Skill 3 - Critique of AI-generated summary

Ask AI to produce a short summary of the dispute, then critique whether it turns allegations into facts, overlooks uncertainty, or suggests advice that should be left to the supervising solicitor.

## AI Section Completion Standard

A complete AI Skills Section should show that you can use AI to help organise interview preparation while still checking every point against the exhibits, preserving uncertainty, avoiding unsupported conclusions, and keeping legal judgment and supervisor review human-led.
