**Interviewing a Patient About a Private Clinic Complaint After a Cosmetic Procedure**

**Scenario Title**

Interviewing a Patient About a Private Clinic Complaint After a Cosmetic Procedure

**Industry / Practice Area**

Healthcare / private clinic complaint / professional negligence-adjacent factual inquiry

## Scenario Background

Harriet Cole had a private cosmetic skin treatment at a clinic and says she suffered burns and scarring. The clinic says redness and discomfort were explained as risks and that Harriet missed a follow-up appointment. Harriet wants the firm to accuse the clinic of negligence and demand compensation. The materials suggest possible consent and aftercare issues, but also gaps around photographs, medical advice, timeline, and prior skin condition. The student must prepare an empathetic client interview that gathers facts without promising a claim outcome.

## Student Role

You are acting as a trainee solicitor or junior legal professional preparing for an initial client interview. Your task is to organise the information, prepare a structured interview, avoid premature advice, and support the supervising solicitor after the interview.

Conduct a structured client interview and produce a short post-interview summary for the supervising solicitor.
