*Analysing a Venue Cancellation and Refund Dispute*

**Scenario Title** Analysing a Venue Cancellation and Refund Dispute

**Industry / Practice Area** Hospitality / Events / Commercial Services

**Difficulty** Intermediate

**Scenario Type** Case and matter analysis simulation

# Scenario Background

Northbank Events Ltd has asked for help with cancelled corporate training event and disputed venue charges. Maya Rafiq, Managing Director, wants the legal team to help recover the deposit, resist late cancellation charges, and preserve the client relationship if possible. The client has a board meeting tomorrow and wants a clear recommendation before deciding whether to send an aggressive demand email. The available file is incomplete and contains conflicting accounts, so the student must analyse the matter carefully before any external communication or recommendation is made.

Central tension: Northbank says the venue cancelled first, while the venue says Northbank delayed confirming final numbers and catering requirements. The matter cannot be resolved by accepting either side's account without further evidence.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal professional in the team. You report to Priya Desai. You are not expected to make final strategic decisions or send external correspondence. Your task is to analyse the matter file, organise the evidence, identify strengths and weaknesses, and prepare a clear note for supervisor review.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 2 - Chronology of Key Events

1. Exhibit 4 - Relevant Agreement or Policy Extract

# Core Skill Task

Analyse a realistic matter file, identify the key issues and evidence gaps, and prepare a practical case/matter analysis note for a supervising solicitor or matter lead.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare a concise case/matter analysis note for Priya Desai. Your note should summarise the client objective, key facts, issue list, strengths, weaknesses, evidence gaps, communication risks, recommended next steps, and decisions requiring supervisor input.
