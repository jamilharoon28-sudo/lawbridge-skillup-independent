**Interviewing a Charity Trustee About Restricted Funds and Internal Pressure**

**Scenario Title**

Interviewing a Charity Trustee About Restricted Funds and Internal Pressure

**Industry / Practice Area**

Charity governance / donor restrictions / internal dispute and professional judgement

## Scenario Background

Adam Reed is treasurer of a small community charity. The charity is short of cash and some trustees want to use money donated for a youth mentoring project to cover rent and payroll. Adam is worried the funds may be restricted, but the chair says “we can sort the wording later.” Documents show donor emails, board pressure, budget gaps, and unclear accounting entries. The student must prepare for a client interview that clarifies authority, restrictions, urgency, and escalation without giving final governance advice.

## Student Role

You are acting as a trainee solicitor or junior legal professional preparing for an initial client interview. Your task is to organise the information, prepare a structured interview, avoid premature advice, and support the supervising solicitor after the interview.

Conduct a structured client interview and produce a short post-interview summary for the supervising solicitor. Your final output should identify what is known, what remains uncertain, what documents are needed, what the client wants, and what should be escalated before any advice or external communication is given.
