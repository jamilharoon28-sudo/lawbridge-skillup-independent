# AI Skills

### AI Skill 1 — Fact/assertion separator

Use AI to separate Noah’s assertions from documents and third-party messages, then correct any overstatements.

### AI Skill 2 — Interview question sensitivity

Ask AI to draft questions about the alleged physical contact, then edit to avoid accusatory or leading wording.

### AI Skill 3 — Meeting preparation structure

Use AI to create a preparation checklist for the investigation meeting, then identify items requiring supervisor review.

### AI Section Completion Standard

A good response uses AI to organise, test and improve preparation, but checks every AI output against the exhibits, preserves uncertainty, and leaves legal judgment and external communication decisions for supervised human review.
