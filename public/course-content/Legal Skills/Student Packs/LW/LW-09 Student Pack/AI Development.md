# AI Skills

## AI Skill 1 - Simplify and structure

Use AI to create a clear first-pass structure for the final communication. Then check whether the structure answers the reader’s actual question and whether it omits any important caveats.

## AI Skill 2 - Tone and overstatement check

Use AI to identify wording that is too aggressive, too vague, too defensive, or unsupported by the exhibits. Decide which suggestions to accept and which require human judgment.

## AI Skill 3 - Fact and assumption audit

Ask AI to separate confirmed facts from assumptions in your draft. Check the AI output against the exhibits and correct any invented or overstated point.

## AI Section Completion Standard

A strong response uses AI to improve structure, tone, and fact organisation while keeping human responsibility for judgment, accuracy, caveats, confidentiality, and final wording.
