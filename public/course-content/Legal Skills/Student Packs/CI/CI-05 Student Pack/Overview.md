**Scenario Title**

Interviewing a Construction Subcontractor About Non-Payment and Alleged Delay

**Industry / Practice Area**

Construction / subcontractor payment dispute / commercial relationship risk

## Scenario Background

Danvers Electrical Ltd completed electrical works on a hospitality refurbishment project. The main contractor has withheld the final invoice, alleging late completion and defective work. Sofia Danvers says her team was delayed by others and wants to threaten suspension on another live project. Site records show some access problems, a disputed variation, and a defect snagging list. The student must prepare for a commercially focused client interview that clarifies dates, documents, and objectives before any external communication.

## Student Role

You are acting as a trainee solicitor or junior legal professional preparing for an initial client interview. Your task is to organise the information, prepare a structured interview, avoid premature advice, and support the supervising solicitor after the interview.
