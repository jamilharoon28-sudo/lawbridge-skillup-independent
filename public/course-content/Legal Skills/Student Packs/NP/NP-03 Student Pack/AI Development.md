# AI Skills

AI Skill 1 - Use AI to create a first-pass negotiation issue map from the exhibits, then correct any unsupported assumptions or missing evidence.

AI Skill 2 - Ask AI to generate possible opening positions, fallback positions, and non-financial concessions, then decide which options require supervisor approval.

AI Skill 3 - Critique an AI-generated negotiation email for overstatement, aggressive tone, invented facts, or concessions that go beyond HarbourGate's authority.

AI Section Completion Standard:

A good response uses AI as a structuring and checking tool only. It verifies all AI outputs against the exhibits, identifies missing evidence, preserves human professional judgment, and avoids allowing AI to decide strategy, invent facts, or make concessions.
