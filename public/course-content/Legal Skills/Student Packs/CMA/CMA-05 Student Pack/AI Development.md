# AI Skills

## AI Skill 1

Use AI to create a first-pass issue map from the six exhibits. Then review it and identify at least three points the AI might miss or overstate in this Consumer Goods / Product Safety / Retail matter.

## AI Skill 2

Ask AI to separate confirmed facts, client assertions, disputed facts, and missing evidence. Check the output against the exhibits and correct any unsupported assumptions.

## AI Skill 3

Use AI to draft a neutral supervisor-facing summary of the matter. Then revise it so it avoids premature conclusions, unsupported blame, and the risky wording in the client's proposed communication.

## AI Section Completion Standard

A good student uses AI to organise and test thinking, but checks every output against the exhibits. The student must correct unsupported assumptions, identify missing evidence, and retain human professional judgment over risk, strategy, wording, and escalation.
