# AI Skills

## AI Skill 1 - Generate research terms and issue map

Use AI to generate search terms and a first-pass issue map. Check whether the output matches the supervisor request and remove irrelevant or jurisdiction-specific suggestions.

## AI Skill 2 - Source extract summary check

Use AI to summarise the provided source extracts. Then compare the summary against the exhibits and correct any overstatement, invented rule, or missing caveat.

## AI Skill 3 - Hallucination and authority check

Ask AI to draft a short legal research conclusion, then identify any unsupported citation, invented legal test, or conclusion not grounded in the provided materials.

## AI Section Completion Standard

A strong response uses AI to organise issues and summarise source extracts, but checks every proposition against the materials, removes invented authorities, and keeps the final research judgment human-led.
