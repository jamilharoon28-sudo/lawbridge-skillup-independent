# AI Skills

### AI Skill 1 — AI empathy wording

Ask AI to draft a neutral opening script for the interview, then edit it to avoid implying fault before evidence is reviewed.

### AI Skill 2 — Photo evidence organiser

Use AI to design a photo chronology table for Harriet’s images, identifying what metadata or confirmation is needed.

### AI Skill 3 — Flawed summary critique

Critique an AI summary that assumes the clinic caused permanent scarring and rewrite it with appropriate caution.

### AI Section Completion Standard

A good response uses AI to organise, test and improve preparation, but checks every AI output against the exhibits, preserves uncertainty, and leaves legal judgment and external communication decisions for supervised human review.
