**Scenario Title**

Writing an Internal Memo About a Risky Employee Reference Request

**Industry / Practice Area**

Employment / Professional Services

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Internal Memo

# Scenario Background

A supervising partner needs an internal memo before responding to a reference request about a former payroll manager. The business wants to be helpful, but internal documents include performance concerns, an unresolved complaint, and a draft reference that may be too positive.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal team member. You have been asked to prepare a professional written communication using the six exhibits. Your role is to organise the information, write clearly for the intended audience, and avoid unsupported conclusions.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Supervisor Request
1. Exhibit 2 - Reference Request
1. Exhibit 3 - HR Exit Note
1. Exhibit 4 - Payroll Error Summary
1. Exhibit 5 - Internal Partner Email
1. Exhibit 6 - Draft Reference

# Core Skill Task

Write a clear, accurate, audience-appropriate internal memo that uses the documents carefully and reflects uncertainty where needed.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare the final internal memo for supervising partner. The communication should be concise, supervisor-facing, risk-aware, and neutral and should help the reader understand the practical next step.
