# Scenario Title:

Reviewing a FinTech Onboarding Partnership With Revenue Share Terms

# Industry / Practice Area:

Financial technology / customer onboarding / strategic partnership

# Scenario Background:

PayBridge Markets is considering whether to launch a revenue-share onboarding partnership. The potential value or exposure is approximately USD 1.1 million expected first-year revenue contribution. The commercial team wants a practical answer by the partnership approval forum on Friday. The business sees opportunity, but the materials show legal, operational, financial, and communication risks. The key tension is that the pilot improved speed but produced unresolved exception cases and unclear responsibility for failed checks The learner must prepare commercial legal analysis that helps the business decide what to do next without overstating the evidence or ignoring commercial priorities.

You are a junior legal team member assisting the supervising solicitor. You have been asked to review the documents, identify the legal and commercial risks, prepare a business-facing analysis, and support a discussion with the business stakeholder. You are not expected to make the final business decision.
