**Scenario Title**

Writing a Case Update for a Family Business Shareholder Dispute

**Industry / Practice Area**

Family Business / Company Administration

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Case Update Email

# Scenario Background

A minority shareholder wants a clear update after receiving incomplete accounts and tense family messages. She wants a strong letter, but the evidence is incomplete and sensitive.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal team member. You have been asked to prepare a professional written communication using the six exhibits. Your role is to organise the information, write clearly for the intended audience, and avoid unsupported conclusions.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Client Request
1. Exhibit 2 - Accounts Extract
1. Exhibit 3 - Dividend History
1. Exhibit 4 - Family Message Thread
1. Exhibit 5 - Supervisor Instruction
1. Exhibit 6 - Priya Draft Reply

# Core Skill Task

Write a clear, accurate, audience-appropriate case update email that uses the documents carefully and reflects uncertainty where needed.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare the final case update email for anxious client. The communication should be reassuring, clear, careful, and practical and should help the reader understand the practical next step.
