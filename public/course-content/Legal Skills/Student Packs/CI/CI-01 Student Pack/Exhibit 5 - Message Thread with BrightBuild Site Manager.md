# Exhibit 5 — Message Thread with BrightBuild Site Manager

**Document type** Extract from client phone messages

**Participants** Amina Patel and Mark Ellis, BrightBuild site manager

**8 April, 18:14 - Client** Hi Mark, can we still add a pull-out pantry next to the fridge space? If it is too expensive leave it, but it would really help.

**8 April, 18:39 - Mark** Should be fine. I will check with office. Might add a bit but we can sort.

**18 April, 17:02 - Client** Cabinet doors still not straight and water under the sink again. Please can someone fix before completion?

**18 April, 17:24 - Mark** Yes snagging. We always come back for adjustments.

**30 April, 16:11 - Mark** Need signature today so office can close the job and schedule any final snags. It does not stop us coming back.

**30 April, 16:18 - Client** I do not want to sign if it means I accept everything is finished.

**30 April, 16:22 - Mark** No stress. Just admin. Office needs it for payment and snagging slot.

**3 May, 10:05 - Client** Invoice includes 950 pantry charge. I was not told the price. Also sink still leaking.

**3 May, 11:36 - Mark** You asked for pantry and we fitted it. Need office to confirm. I thought price was discussed but maybe not by me.

**Interview significance** The messages support the need for careful questioning about consent, pressure, defects, practical completion, and what the client understood when signing.
