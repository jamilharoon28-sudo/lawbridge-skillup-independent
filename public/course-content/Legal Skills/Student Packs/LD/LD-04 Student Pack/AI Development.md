# AI Skills

## AI Skill 1 — Structure and judgment

Use AI to create a first-pass structure for the data handling addendum and approval paragraph for a marketing platform renewal. Then identify at least three places where human legal judgment or supervisor approval is still required.

## AI Skill 2 — Risk review

Ask AI to identify ambiguous or risky wording in a draft relating to Orchid Lane Homeware and PulseSend Marketing Ltd. Check whether the AI invents facts, assumes legal rules, or overlooks uncertainty.

## AI Skill 3 — Tone control

Use AI to produce two alternative tones for one key paragraph: one firm and one collaborative. Decide which is safer for this scenario and explain why.

## AI Section Completion Standard:

A good answer uses AI only as a drafting support tool, checks all AI output against the exhibits, identifies assumptions and missing information, avoids jurisdiction-specific legal conclusions, and preserves final judgment for the student and supervisor.
