**Interviewing an E-Commerce Founder After Customer Complaints and a Payment Freeze**

**Scenario Title**

Interviewing an E-Commerce Founder After Customer Complaints and a Payment Freeze

**Industry / Practice Area**

E-commerce / product complaints / payment processor and reputational risk

## Scenario Background

Priya Shaw sells home products online. A payment processor has frozen part of BlueNest Goods’ account after customer complaints about overheating heated blankets. Priya says the complaints are exaggerated and wants to reassure customers publicly. The documents show product complaints, a supplier statement, payment processor queries, and a draft customer email that may understate safety concerns. The student must prepare to interview Priya about facts, evidence, product safety, customer communications, and immediate escalation needs.

## Student Role

You are acting as a trainee solicitor or junior legal professional preparing for an initial client interview. Your task is to organise the information, prepare a structured interview, avoid premature advice, and support the supervising solicitor after the interview.
