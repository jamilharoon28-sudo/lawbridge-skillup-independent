# Scenario Title:

Evaluating a Construction Materials Price Escalation Demand

# Industry / Practice Area:

Construction supply / infrastructure / pricing risk

# Scenario Background:

MetroSpan Infrastructure Ltd is considering whether to accept, reject, or negotiate a supplier price increase. The potential value or exposure is approximately USD 2.4 million potential cost increase. The commercial team wants a practical answer by the project steering group tomorrow afternoon. The business sees opportunity, but the materials show legal, operational, financial, and communication risks. The key tension is that the supply contract has price adjustment wording but the formula and evidence pack are incomplete The learner must prepare commercial legal analysis that helps the business decide what to do next without overstating the evidence or ignoring commercial priorities.

# Student Role:

You are a junior legal team member assisting the supervising solicitor. You have been asked to review the documents, identify the legal and commercial risks, prepare a business-facing analysis, and support a discussion with the business stakeholder. You are not expected to make the final business decision.

1. Exhibit 1 - Business Instruction Email

1. Exhibit 3 - Operational Data and Timeline Note
1. Exhibit 4 - Internal Stakeholder Message Thread

1. Exhibit 6 - Draft Board or Customer Communication

# Core Skill Task:

Analyse a commercial problem through a legal lens and translate the risks into practical business options, safeguards, and next steps.

# Main Practical Task:

Prepare a commercial legal risk note for Liam Grant, explaining the key risks, options, safeguards, missing evidence, and recommended next steps on aggregate supply price escalation demand. Use the note to support a business briefing conversation.
