# Scenario Title:

Analysing a Stadium Catering Outsourcing Proposal Under Time Pressure

# Industry / Practice Area:

Sports and events / outsourcing / food service operations

# Scenario Background:

Cityside Arena Group is considering whether to outsource catering before the next season. The potential value or exposure is approximately USD 3.2 million annual catering contract. The commercial team wants a practical answer by the stadium operations board on Monday. The business sees opportunity, but the materials show legal, operational, financial, and communication risks. The key tension is that eventbite promises higher revenue share but mobilisation planning, staff transfer issues, and service-level remedies are incomplete The learner must prepare commercial legal analysis that helps the business decide what to do next without overstating the evidence or ignoring commercial priorities.

# Student Role:

You are a junior legal team member assisting the supervising solicitor. You have been asked to review the documents, identify the legal and commercial risks, prepare a business-facing analysis, and support a discussion with the business stakeholder. You are not expected to make the final business decision.

# Documents / Information Available:

1. Exhibit 1 - Business Instruction Email
1. Exhibit 2 - Proposed Commercial Terms Extract
1. Exhibit 3 - Operational Data and Timeline Note
1. Exhibit 4 - Internal Stakeholder Message Thread
1. Exhibit 5 - Risk, Finance, and Options Note
1. Exhibit 6 - Draft Board or Customer Communication

# Core Skill Task:

Analyse a commercial problem through a legal lens and translate the risks into practical business options, safeguards, and next steps.

# Main Practical Task:

Prepare a commercial legal risk note for Rachel Voss, explaining the key risks, options, safeguards, missing evidence, and recommended next steps on stadium catering outsourcing proposal. Use the note to support a business briefing conversation.
