# Scenario Title:

Negotiating a Service Remedy After Dental Equipment Downtime

# Industry / Practice Area:

Healthcare operations / equipment servicing

# Scenario Background:

Briar Dental Studio is preparing for a negotiation with Meditron Equipment Services about a equipment service failure and compensation dispute. The client wants to secure urgent repair assurance, service continuity, and a proportionate financial remedy without overstating unverified losses. The business is under time pressure because the issue needs movement by before the clinic sends a termination notice. The documents show useful leverage, but they also reveal uncertainty, evidence gaps, and facts that may weaken the client's position. The task is to prepare a balanced negotiation plan that protects the client's position without overstating liability or creating unnecessary relationship damage.

# Student Role:

You are a junior legal team member assisting the supervising solicitor. You have been asked to review the documents, prepare the negotiation strategy, identify missing evidence, and support a live negotiation. You are not expected to make final settlement decisions without supervisor approval.

# Documents / Information Available:

1. Exhibit 1 - Client Instruction Email
1. Exhibit 2 - Contract or Terms Extract
1. Exhibit 3 - Chronology and Evidence Summary
1. Exhibit 4 - Counterparty Position Note
1. Exhibit 5 - Commercial Risk and Cost Note
1. Exhibit 6 - Draft Negotiation Email Prepared by Client

# Core Skill Task:

Prepare for a negotiation by identifying objectives, leverage, risks, fallback positions, concessions, and communication boundaries.

# Main Practical Task:

Prepare a negotiation strategy note for the supervising solicitor and use it to conduct a focused negotiation with Meditron. After the negotiation, record the agreed position, unresolved points, and recommended next steps.
