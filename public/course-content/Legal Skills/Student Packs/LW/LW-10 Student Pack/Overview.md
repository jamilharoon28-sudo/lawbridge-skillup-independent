**Scenario Title**

Writing a Written Explanation About a Car Finance Dispute

**Industry / Practice Area**

Consumer Finance / Vehicle Purchase

**Difficulty**

Intermediate

**Final output**

Written Explanation And Next Steps Note

# Scenario Background

A client financed a used van for courier work. He wants a simple written explanation of his options after repairs failed. The documents show faults, finance involvement, and unclear inspection evidence.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal team member. You have been asked to prepare a professional written communication using the six exhibits. Your role is to organise the information, write clearly for the intended audience, and avoid unsupported conclusions.

# Documents / Information Available

1. Exhibit 1 - Client Request
1. Exhibit 2 - Vehicle Timeline
1. Exhibit 3 - Finance Agreement Extract
1. Exhibit 4 - Dealer Email
1. Exhibit 5 - Mechanic Text
1. Exhibit 6 - Daniel Draft Message

# Core Skill Task

Write a clear, accurate, audience-appropriate written explanation and next steps note that uses the documents carefully and reflects uncertainty where needed.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare the final written explanation and next steps note for consumer client. The communication should be plain-English, structured, realistic, and careful and should help the reader understand the practical next step.
