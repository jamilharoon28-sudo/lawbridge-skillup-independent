**Interviewing an Influencer Agency Founder About a Failed Brand Campaign**

**Scenario Title**

Interviewing an Influencer Agency Founder About a Failed Brand Campaign

**Industry / Practice Area**

Media / influencer marketing / commercial contract and reputational risk

## Scenario Background

Ana Petrova runs a small influencer agency. A skincare brand refuses to pay the final campaign fee, alleging late content, poor disclosure tags, and failure to meet performance targets. Ana says the brand changed the brief and approved the posts. The materials show mixed approval messages, unclear performance obligations, and a draft public response that may escalate reputational risk. The student must prepare for an interview that clarifies objectives, evidence, and communication boundaries.

## Student Role

You are acting as a trainee solicitor or junior legal professional preparing for an initial client interview. Your task is to organise the information, prepare a structured interview, avoid premature advice, and support the supervising solicitor after the interview.
