*Analysing a Product Safety Complaint and Retailer Response*

**Scenario Title** Analysing a Product Safety Complaint and Retailer Response

**Industry / Practice Area** Consumer Goods / Product Safety / Retail

**Difficulty** Intermediate

**Scenario Type** Case and matter analysis simulation

# Scenario Background

Evervale Baby Products Ltd has asked for help with customer injury complaint involving a folding travel cot supplied through a retailer. Leah Whitmore, Commercial Director, wants the legal team to help map the case file, identify evidence gaps and recommend immediate handling steps. A retailer is considering removing the product from its website and the client wants to issue a defensive statement. The available file is incomplete and contains conflicting accounts, so the student must analyse the matter carefully before any external communication or recommendation is made.

Central tension: The customer alleges a hinge failed during normal use, but the factory report suggests possible incorrect assembly. The matter cannot be resolved by accepting either side's account without further evidence.

# Student Role

You are acting as a junior legal professional in the team. You report to Samira Khan. You are not expected to make final strategic decisions or send external correspondence. Your task is to analyse the matter file, organise the evidence, identify strengths and weaknesses, and prepare a clear note for supervisor review.

# Core Skill Task

Analyse a realistic matter file, identify the key issues and evidence gaps, and prepare a practical case/matter analysis note for a supervising solicitor or matter lead.

# Main Practical Task

Prepare a concise case/matter analysis note for Samira Khan. Your note should summarise the client objective, key facts, issue list, strengths, weaknesses, evidence gaps, communication risks, recommended next steps, and decisions requiring supervisor input.
