# Scenario Title:

Reviewing Commercial Risk in a Customer Data Migration Project

# Industry / Practice Area:

Technology / SaaS migration / customer operations

# Scenario Background:

Northbank Fitness Clubs is considering whether to approve a compressed migration timetable. The potential value or exposure is approximately USD 640,000 subscription and implementation spend. The commercial team wants a practical answer by the launch committee meeting on Wednesday. The business sees opportunity, but the materials show legal, operational, financial, and communication risks. The key tension is that northbank has not completed data cleansing and memberflow has limited liability for customer data issues The learner must prepare commercial legal analysis that helps the business decide what to do next without overstating the evidence or ignoring commercial priorities.

# Student Role:

You are a junior legal team member assisting the supervising solicitor. You have been asked to review the documents, identify the legal and commercial risks, prepare a business-facing analysis, and support a discussion with the business stakeholder. You are not expected to make the final business decision.

# Core Skill Task:

Analyse a commercial problem through a legal lens and translate the risks into practical business options, safeguards, and next steps.

# Main Practical Task:

Prepare a commercial legal risk note for Ravi Menon, explaining the key risks, options, safeguards, missing evidence, and recommended next steps on customer membership platform migration. Use the note to support a business briefing conversation.
